YOUR SUPPORT MAKES AN IMPACT!

Serving over 43,000 children and caregivers every year, the Schoolhouse Children’s Museum is a unique area resource designed specifically for young children and their families. As an independent nonprofit organization, the SCM relies on our community of donors and supporters to create meaningful, relevant, engaging, and educational experiences.

Support from public and private funders covers more than 57% of the Museum’s annual expenses. Your support ensures our ability to inspire and engage children and families to explore, play, learn and discover through creative, interactive opportunities and experiences that integrate the arts, humanities, and sciences.

Your gift helps the Schoolhouse Children’s Museum to:

  • Provide an enriching, safe and clean environment for our young guests of all abilities
  • Prioritize affordability to give every child access to play
  • Serve school groups and community organizations with creative, hands-on play experiences
  • Provide free Focused Field Trips for Title One Schools and discounted admission to families who hold EBT cards.

GIFT PLANNING

Help shape the future with a planned gift!

Why consider a Planned Gift?

Through a planned gift to the Museum you can leave a legacy that will benefit thousands of children at a transformative time in their life where thinking and creativity bloom – early childhood.

Planned Gifts can complement your financial goals and provide tax benefits. Options for Gift Planning include:

  • Bequest
  • Gift Annuity
  • Life Insurance/retirement accounts
  • Deferred gift annuity
  • Lead trusts
  • Remainder trusts
  • Securities

Join the Schoolhouse Discover Society

The Schoolhouse Discover Society honors those forward-thinkers who’ve chosen to include the Schoolhouse Children’s Museum in their estate plan.

If you’ve already designated the Schoolhouse Children’s Museum as a beneficiary in any of yourplanning documents, we respectfully request that you let us know. Your early notification allows us to honor your generosity and ensure that your gift is used in a manner that aligns with your intentions.
