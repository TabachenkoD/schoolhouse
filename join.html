Membership

Overview

Membership is so much more than unlimited admission—it's your family's all-access pass to explore, play, learn and discover in a setting where history comes to life! Take advantage of exclusive privileges such as discounts on classes, programs, birthday parties, and gift shop. Plus, your membership helps support our educational programs, ensuring that children have access to enriching experiences that inspire a lifelong love of learning. Join today and turn every visit into an adventure at the Schoolhouse Children’s Museum!

Please note: There is a possibility the Museum will close this fall for renovations. In such an event, memberships would be extended for the duration of the closure.

Memberships Include

  1. Unlimited complimentary admission to the Schoolhouse Museum for one year
  2. 10% discount on merchandise at the Schoolhouse Gift Shop
  3. 10% discount on birthday party packages
  4. Discount on education classes and special events
  5. A first-look at upcoming events, programs, new exhibits and more via Schoolhouse e-news

Three ways to become a member

  1. Online: Use the buttons below to sign up today.
  2. Over the Phone: Call our membership team at 561-742-6785.
  3. In-Person: Download the registration form and bring it to the front desk on your next visit.

Family

A family membership allows for unlimited play for one year for the family! Review the options below to determine which fit is best for you.

Family Basic $100

Family Basic Membership benefits

  1. Unlimited visits for 2 named adults and up to 5 legally dependent children under age 18 living in the same household (names & ages for children/IDs for adults required; at least one adult must live in the household)
  2. Admission to FREE weekly education programs (space permitting)
  3. 10% discount on Birthday Parties
  4. 10% on merchandise in the Gift Shop
  5. Discount on education classes and special events
  6. Two (2) Admit 1 FREE passes for one-time use

Family Plus $120

All Family Basic Membership benefits, plus

  • 1 additional named adult (must be 18 years of age or older)
  • All legally dependent named children under age 18 living in the same household
  • Two additional (total of 4) Admit 1 FREE passes for one-time use

Family Premium $175

All Family Basic Membership benefits, plus

  • 1 additional named adult (must be 18 years of age or older)
  • All legally dependent named children under age 18 living in the same household
  • Two additional (total of 6) Admit 1 FREE passes for one-time use
  • Association of Children’s Museum (ACM) benefits providing 50% off general admission for up to six (6) people to over 200 additional children’s museum nationwide in compliance with the ACM Reciprocal Agreement. Bring your membership card with the red RN logo to receive this discount. For a complete list of ACM approved children’s museum, click the red RN logo below

Grandparent

A grandparent membership allows for unlimited play for one year for named grandparents and up to five (5) grandchildren! Review the options below to determine which fit is best for you.

Grandparent Basic $65

Grandparent Basic Membership benefits

  • Unlimited visits for 2 named grandparents and up to 5 named grandchildren
  • Admission to FREE weekly education programs (space permitting)
  • 10% discount on Birthday Parties
  • 10% on merchandise in the Gift Shop
  • Preferential pricing on specialty workshops, education classes and special events
  • Priority registration for specialty workshops, education classes and special events

Grandparent Premium $115

All Grandparent Basic benefits, plus

  • Two (2) Admit 1 FREE passes for one-time use.
  • Association of Children’s Museum (ACM) benefits providing 50% off general admission for up to six (6) people to over 200 additional children’s museum nationwide in compliance with the ACM Reciprocal Agreement.
    For a complete list of ACM approved children’s museum, visit childrensmuseum.org
