Volunteer

Join our team of volunteers and "bring learning to life!"

Discover how you can become part of our passionate team and contribute to making a difference in our community.

Teen Volunteers

The Schoolhouse Children's Museum is an ideal environment for teens to gain leadership skills, work experience and fulfill needed hours to meet school requirements. Teen Volunteers are an important part of the Schoolhouse Children's Museum and help us provide the best experience for members and visitors.

We are currently accepting applications. All applicants are asked to complete an application (below) and go through a brief interview process.

As a teen volunteer, ages 15-17, you have the opportunity to lead hands-on activities, heighten the experience of children and families by interacting with visitors at our exhibits, assist the staff in classes and with birthday parties, and volunteer at special events.

Adult Volunteers

College students, parents and grandparents have a wealth of knowledge to share. Whether guiding children through exhibits, leading an interactive craft or assisting in a class, adult volunteers serve as excellent museum interpreters and are an important part of our team!

Volunteer Opportunities

Classroom Volunteer

Creative volunteers are needed to assist the Museum staff with hands-on activities in our classes for museum visitors, school groups and our off-site Outreach Program.
Hours: Programs are regularly scheduled Monday-Saturday

Special Event Volunteer

From months before to the day of the event, many hands are needed to support our special events. Help is always needed on Event Day- from set-up and clean-up to the many tasks in between - Come join the fun!

Community Liaison

Staff a booth at local fairs and exhibits.

Museum Handyman

Your experience can help maintain and repair our exhibits. We often have a “honey-do” list of projects that could use your expertise.
Hours: Flexible; as needed basis

Or share your time and talents in a variety of ways...Photographer? Help document our events with photos & videos ...Makeup Artist or Face Painter? ...Puppeteer?

Come join us!
