Party

Let your Adventure Begin!

Whether Museum events, program events and activities, or your own private party, The Schoolhouse Children's Museum is the place to Imagine, Play, Engage, Learn, and Create...
The Schoolhouse Children’s Museum is a unique destination for your next birthday or special occasion!

Our colorful, interactive galleries provide the perfect backdrop for celebrating your special day! We can help you to make your party a memorable and fun occasion for all your guests – parents and children alike! Party hosts have use of the party room for two hours. All party attendees have free play in our award winning museum during your party, accompanied by adults.

All Party Packages Include:

  • Downloadable E-Invitations
  • Complimentary gift registry at our Gift Shop
  • Free parking
  • Museum play during party (children under 18 must be accompanied by their adults)
  • Exclusive use of the Party Room during your designated time
  • A museum staff assistant in the Party Room
  • Birthday balloons
  • One FREE return pass for each child guest

FACILITY RENTALS

Looking for a unique space to hold your next gathering?

The Schoolhouse Children’s Museum offers a bright and interactive atmosphere to make your event memorable! Our museum offers a colorful and engaging ambience that is multifunctional and suited for everything from baby showers to club meetings.

Contact Coordinator Matthew at 561-742-6786 to see if your date is available.

Multiple spaces may be combined to create the perfect space to fit your needs. (Please note: The entire museum is not available during regular business hours)

Facility Rental Packages:

Entire Museum

The award winning Schoolhouse Children’s Museum offers two floors of unique and versatile space. Our imaginative exhibits will lend an interactive component to your event, making the experience one of a kind.

This option is available year round on Sundays and Mondays.

Classroom Only

Our Classroom is a truly multifunctional event space. A perfect space for you to host a business or club meeting. Available during operating hours as well as after hours.
Does not include playtime in the Museum.

All facility rentals include:

  • On-site parking
  • Handicap accessibility
  • Projector access
  • Tables and chairs
  • Staff member on site
