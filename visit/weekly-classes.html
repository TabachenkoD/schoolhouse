Weekly Classes

At the Schoolhouse, we are proud to be more than just a Museum! We offer an array of educational programming for children ages 18 months - 7 years.
These developmentally appropriate, interactive programs focus on your child’s developmental growth all with an eye towards future academic success.

We feature hands-on, sensory based exploration and play intertwined with a literature, art, science, music and/or dance focus.
All programs encourage learning - meeting the child where they are - and allowing for individuality while developing social skills and group readiness.

Classes for All Ages

Kid's Music Circle with Noam Brown

Music Specialist Noam Brown leads the fully participatory class with live guitar and joyful singing, rhythm, and movement.

Classes for 2-5 Year Olds:

Sensational Story ‘n More

Instructors bring children’s books to life through interactive performance, song, movement & props. An engaging art activity complements every class.

Messy Play

Let your little ones explore while developing important sensory skills - skills that build early scientific inquiry and creativity. Each class features multiple activities based around a common theme. Come play with us!

Tots in Tutus with First Steps Dance & Tumbling

First Steps Dance & Tumbling instructor Miss Amy Norris teaches this energetic movement and ballet class for ages 2 - 5.

Tumble Tots with First Steps Dance & Tumbling

Join us for a 30-minute movement and tumbling class for boys and girls.

Art Explorations

A Preschool Art class where children will explore the elements of art in a process-based format.

Yoga Fun!

Children’s Yoga teacher Karen Lampert graciously donates her time, offering FREE classes. LIMIT 10 on a first come basis.

Classes 3 - 5 Year Olds:

ABC's & 123's

A preschool/kindergarten readiness class for young children. Must sign up for whole series.

Preschool Science

Come explore basic preschool science principles with in this hands-on program

Classes For 3 - 7 Year Olds:

The Art of the Story

Featuring one author/illustrator at each class, children will create their own art in the style of the featured book.
