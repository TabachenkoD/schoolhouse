Weekly Classes

At the Schoolhouse, we are proud to be more than just a Museum! We offer an array of educational programming for children ages 18 months - 7 years.
These developmentally appropriate, interactive programs focus on your child’s developmental growth all with an eye towards future academic success.

We feature hands-on, sensory based exploration and play intertwined with a literature, art, science, music and/or dance focus.
All programs encourage learning - meeting the child where they are - and allowing for individuality while developing social skills and group readiness.
