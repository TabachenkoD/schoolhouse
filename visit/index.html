Museums for All

The Schoolhouse Children's Museum is proud to participate in the Museums for All program which expands access to the nation's museum for EBT card holders. This program was made possible by a cooperative venture with the Association of Children's Museums (ACM) and the Institute of Museum and Library Services (IMLS).

The Schoolhouse Children's Museum offers a reduced admission price of $2.00 each for up to 4 family members upon presentation of an EBT card from any state and corresponding ID. Children under 12 months old are free.

To make a reservation for play, please call the Museum at 561-742-6780, let the Visitor Services Associate know you are a Museums for All patron and which day and time you would like to come in. Also let him/her know how many people (including babies under 12 months old) will be in your party. You will pay your admission fee upon arrival at the Museum. Please be sure to present your EBT Card and corresponding ID.

General Information:

  • Minors under 18 years of age may not enter the Museum unless accompanied by an adult.
  • Adults are not allowed in the Museum unless accompanied by a child.
  • We gladly provide tours for unaccompanied adults interested in the Museum, its history and exhibits.
  • The Schoolhouse Children's Museum is wheelchair accessible. For accommodation under the American’s with Disabilities Act, please contact Miriam Naranjo at 561-742-6787 or email Miriam@schoolhousemuseum.org. Please provide five business days’ notice for American Sign Language Interpreter.
  • Service animals are welcome inside the Museum. Pets and companion animals are not allowed inside the Museum.

SCHOOLHOUSE CHILDREN’S MUSEUM ADMISSION POLICY

We appreciate your patience and understanding as we continue to strive for the safety of our patrons. The Schoolhouse is open Tuesdays through Saturdays.

The Museum operates Tuesday through Saturday, at a limited capacity. We highly recommend advanced reservations. The Museum has a limited capacity. Visitors to the Schoolhouse Museum are asked to schedule their visit by reserving the day and time of their visit. If the Museum is full at the time of your arrival and you do not have a reservation, your party will be placed on a waiting list. All visitors and members may obtain an online ticket to enter the Museum . Members receive free ticket registrations for each timed entry window.

ADULTS MUST BE ACCOMPANIED BY A CHILD TO GAIN ENTRANCE TO THE SCHOOLHOUSE CHILDREN’S MUSEUM
