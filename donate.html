The Schoolhouse Children's Museum is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing educational opportunities to young children. Your tax-deductible contribution helps pay for art supplies, special programs, and much more.

You can make a contribution now by completing the fields below or send your check to the museum. Contact us if you need additional documentation for tax purposes.

Please also remember us in your estate plan, will, or life insurance. It is an easy way to leave a legacy and provide educational opportunities to future generations.

As an independent 501c3 nonprofit organization, the Schoolhouse Children’s Museum relies on its generous donors to help make ends meet. We ask for your help in continuing the important work of inspiring young minds and sparking their passion for lifelong and curiosity and learning through play. Please make your gift now, Donate or review giving suggestions below and contact us with any questions you may have.
